About

Mazus Records is an independent record label based in Minneapolis, MN. It was planted around the summer of '69, and sprouted in the summer of 2009.

Our releases deliver high-quality Compact Disc packages along with digital download options. We also offer vinyl releases via the Qrates and Feedbands platforms.


We aim to:

  • Release music we think is deserving, and that wouldn't otherwise become fully grown
  • Follow an album-focused perspective, where there is a *record* of something that's more than the sum of its parts
  • Remain essentially 100% independent
